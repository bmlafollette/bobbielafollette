ABOUT



I am a future web developer in the .NET Language Acceleration Program at Eleven Fifty Academy in Carmel, Indiana.

A brief history about me … as a straight 'A' student near the top of my high school class, I could have been anything I wanted, but I just didn't know what that was. I had a passion for science, math, and art and switched college majors from engineering, to biology, to pharmacy, and back to biology before switching campuses a year later and changing my major to computer graphics. I dabbled in graphic design, web design, and coding in some of my classes, and I loved that I could combine my passion for design and engineering with problem solving. I finished a 2 year degree and started working at a small architecture office using Photoshop and AutoCAD. Unfortunately, that didn't last long because the office closed within a year and I was without a job. Creative jobs were scarce in east central Indiana, so I ended up taking a temporary job at a local bank. That job turned into a promotion, and I went back to school in the evenings to obtain a bachelor of science degree in accounting, which I followed up with sitting for the CPA exam. Looking to put my CPA credentials to use, I left the bank in 2013 to work for a public accounting firm for two years until I was offered an accounting manager position at a small credit union in Indianapolis by a former colleague.

I wish I could say that I enjoyed that job and the one before it, but after doing some thinking and self-reflection, I realized I hadn’t felt satisfied in years because I wasn’t being true to myself and my natural creative talents and tendencies. I did some online searching for ideas of how I could change this and came across Eleven Fifty Academy. I took an introductory coding class and was hooked! I was born to design and engineer, and am looking forward to a satisfying career in web development.
